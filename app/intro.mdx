A [DataComp](https://www.datacomp.ai/) and [Bespoke Labs](https://bespokelabs.ai/) community effort to curate the best open reasoning datasets.

Our latest release is <b>[OpenThoughts3](https://openthoughts.ai/blog/ot3)</b>, the new SOTA open reasoning data recipe. Read the [blog](https://openthoughts.ai/blog/ot3) and the full [paper](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2506.04178) for more detail!
